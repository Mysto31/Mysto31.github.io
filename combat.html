Combat

In Bedwars, combat is one of the most important things. Not only does it play a tremendous part in helping you win, but it is generally good in Minecraft to be good at combat.

While fighting with a sword might seem like a good idea, some situations call for other, more expensive weapons in the Bedwars shop such as Fireballs or Bows and some Arrows.
